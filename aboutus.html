About Us

Green Thumbs was established in 2022. Green Thumbs was created to tackles the lack of nutrition or food due to people unable to afford food. It does this by collecting and donating vegatable seeds to people in need.

Green Thumbs is a international organization which was founded in South Africa which has helped over 1 million people all over the world. It was founded by Ayanad Mantshinga and Louis Amphibian. Their vision was to grow food in differnt areas and homes for the less fortunate. The idea came by when people lost thier jobs due to the Covid Lockdown and people unable to pay for thier basic needs like food. From that revolutionary idea, the Green Thumbs was born on Aug. 17, 2022. Within weeks, the foundation began reshaping

The results of this unique program have been ground-breaking, improving people's lives. Over 1 million people have been helped.

We work to change policy as well as hearts and minds on plant at a time.

Here is a testimonial from a community in Queens Town featured in Daily sun local newspaper: "Thanks to Green THumbs, we have been able to provide for the needs of more children and their families here in Queens Town"

The threat of malnutrition and lack of food is slowly increasing, due Green Thumbs we hope to tackle this problem completely so no one has to wake up or sleep with out food in their stomach

Poverty, Homelessness, Injustice End Here! If you believe that every life can change, we have got more to share!
